Rules and Scoring

How the pool works

Each team has one S Rider and five support riders. Riders earn points throughout the Tour de France based on stage finishes, daily jersey holders, final jersey winners, and final general classification results.

The goal is to keep daily racing exciting, so stage finishes are the biggest recurring source of points.

Stage Finish Points

Points are awarded each stage to riders who finish in the top 10.

Inactive and Spare Riders

Spare riders are listed in the rider pool but do not score points unless the group agrees to activate them. Abandoned or injured riders stop earning points once they are no longer racing.
